SmartLMS is a secure, next-generation learning platform designed for modern education. We focus on academic integrity, student engagement, and providing educators with the tools they need to succeed in a digital-first world.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Students

How do I take my quizzes?
Navigate to your enrolled course in the student dashboard and select the available quiz to start. Ensure you have a stable connection.
Where can I find my certificates?
Once you successfully complete a course, you can view and download your verified certificates from the 'My Certificates' section.

For Teachers

How do I monitor student integrity?
You can view real-time security alerts and detailed violation reports in your dashboard for any assessment you've created.
Can I schedule recurring live classes?
Yes, sessions can be configured with recurring schedules when creating or editing a live class in your course.

For Admins

How do I manage system maintenance?
Admins can schedule maintenance windows or enable manual maintenance mode directly from the System Settings dashboard.
How do I create teacher accounts?
Teachers can be invited via email through the 'User Management' section, ensuring they receive the proper permissions.
